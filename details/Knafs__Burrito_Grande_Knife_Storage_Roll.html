Knafs Burrito Grande Knife Storage Roll

A practical and stylish way to store your knives and tools, designed for durability, portability, and easy access.

Why You'll Love It

The Knafs Burrito Grande is the perfect tool for keeping your knives, pens, EDC kits, and other essentials organized. Its expandable design offers plenty of space to fit up to 17 knives and tools, making it suitable for both everyday use and outdoor activities. Whether you're in the kitchen, workshop, or on the go, this storage roll keeps your gear secure and within reach.

Key Features

  • Large expandable design fits up to 17 knives and tools
  • Water-resistant cotton waxed canvas for durability
  • Double-stitched webbing adds extra strength
  • Corner magnets secure contents and prevent tools from falling
  • Includes top handle and latches for easy transport

Perfect for Every Situation

This versatile knife storage roll is ideal for organizing your garage, preparing for a camping trip, or keeping your kitchen tools in order. Its open size of 13.25x29.5 inches provides ample room, and when rolled up, it shrinks down to a compact 13.25x6.5 inches for easy storage in drawers or bags. The built-in elastic tool seatbelts and rigid interior structure make it a reliable choice for keeping your tools in place during transit or disassembly.

A Great Gift for Knife Enthusiasts

If you're someone who values quality, organization, and functionality, the Knafs Burrito Grande is the ideal storage solution. Its combination of durable materials, safe closures, and intuitive design ensures your tools stay protected, accessible, and ready for action whenever you need them.

What Sets It Apart

Unlike traditional knife storage options, the Burrito Grande offers a blend of practicality and portability. It's engineered for everyday use, but also built to last through demanding outdoor conditions. Whether you're storing folding knives, tactical tools, or kitchen essentials, this roll provides a secure, organized, and convenient way to keep everything in one place.
