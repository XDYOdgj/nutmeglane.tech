Parsuko Chef Knife Roll Bag

This chef knife roll bag is an essential tool for anyone who values safe and organized kitchen gear. Made from premium waterproof waxed canvas, it's built to last while keeping your knives protected from moisture, scratches, and damage. The spacious 27.3-inch by 18.7-inch dimensions are designed to securely hold up to nearly 18-inch knives, making it perfect for professionals and home cooks alike.

With 10 well-organized pockets, you can easily sort your knives and other tools. Each compartment is sized to fit various blade lengths, and there are individual slots to keep your knives safe and prevent them from chipping. The lightweight design and adjustable strap make it easy to carry in your pocket or roll along your bag, offering hassle-free portability for your kitchen essentials.
